# MAIN MEADOW DESIGN

## Overview

The Main Meadow is the player's **private home base** - a peaceful, personal space where they begin their journey and return between adventures. Unlike the World Portals (multiplayer), the Meadow is a solo sanctuary.

---

## CORE CONCEPT

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Privacy** | Solo only - no other players visible |
| **Purpose** | Home base, reflection, personal growth |
| **Mood** | Calm, safe, cozy, welcoming |
| **Time of Day** | Matches player's local time (dawn/day/dusk/night) |

## AMBIANCE & ATMOSPHERE

### Time of Day

Meadow reflects player's local time:

| Time | Lighting | Atmosphere |
|------|----------|------------|
| 6am-10am | Dawn, soft pink/orange | Birds chirping, dew on grass |
| 10am-5pm | Bright daylight | Butterflies, active |
| 5pm-8pm | Golden hour, warm | Fireflies starting |
| 8pm-10pm | Dusk, purple/blue | Fireflies everywhere |
| 10pm-6am | Night, moonlit | Stars, crickets, peaceful |

### Sounds

- Gentle wind through grass
- Birds (daytime) / Crickets (night)
- Water trickle (if pond/stream)
- Lumo's happy sounds when following you

### Lumo in Meadow

- Lumo follows you around the meadow
- Can sit with you under the Sticker Tree
- Reacts to areas (excited near portals, cozy near tree)
